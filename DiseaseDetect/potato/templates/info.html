About Leafy

Leafy is an amalgamation of Machine Learning and Web Technology which delivers automated disease detection to any Desktop/Mobile. At the core Leafy uses a Neural Network to process the image through a series of transformations and feature extraction to classify it into one of eleven categories of diseases. The higher levels of the Neural Network perform actions such as edge detection and shape analysis whereas the lower levels are mostly concerned with detection of patterns and anomalies.
In the initial phase the Neural Network was fed with more than 10,000 images of various diseases and trained to recognize them. It was trained continuously for 7 days till the neural network was able to predict the diseases with a fair level of confidence.
After the training was complete the neural network was hooked to a web interface designed for better accessibility by end users. To use it, first the user has to upload the image for prediction. After the necessary processing is completed the name of the disease along with its symptoms and remedies will be shown to the user.
